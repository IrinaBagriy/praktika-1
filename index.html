what is Hurrem Sultan

Hurrem Sultan (original name Alexandra Lisowska), also

known as Roxelana, Roksolana, Roxalene, Roxolane, and Rossa
was born between
1502 to 1506 in Rohatyn, which is known as Ukraine today.
She was the wife of Sultan Suleiman The Magnificient.
Hurrem was the most popular name that fixated with her
due to her cheerful personality. She is considered as one of most powerful women in the Ottoman history.
